Tabster

A powerful tab manager for your browser

Features

Custom Tab Names

Rename your tabs to anything you want, making them easier to identify and organize.

Tab Notes

Add detailed notes to your tabs that appear when you hover over them.

Quick Access

Access the renaming feature from anywhere with a right-click or by clicking the extension icon.

Persistent Storage

Your custom tab names and notes persist even after browser restarts.

How to Use

  1. Quick Access: Click the Tabster icon in your toolbar or right-click anywhere on the page.
  2. Rename Tab: Enter your desired tab name in the "Tab name" field.
  3. Add Notes: Optionally add notes that will appear when you hover near the tab.
  4. Save Changes: Press Enter to save your changes, or click outside the dialog to cancel.
  5. View Notes: Move your mouse to the top of the window to see your tab notes.
